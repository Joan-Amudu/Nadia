Vinyasa Yoga

Experience the fluidity of movement and breath with Vinyasa yoga, a dynamic practice that synchronizes breath with continuous flowing sequences of poses. Led by Nadya, our Vinyasa yoga classes offer a mindful exploration of movement, balance, and strength. Each session is designed to cultivate a harmonious connection between body and mind, as you gracefully transition through poses, building heat, flexibility, and awareness. Whether you're a seasoned practitioner or new to yoga, join us on the mat to awaken your vitality and discover the joy of moving with intention.

Pre-Natal Yoga

Nurture your body and prepare for the miracle of birth with prenatal yoga led by Nadya. Our prenatal yoga classes offer gentle and supportive practices tailored specifically for expectant mothers. Through a combination of gentle stretches, breathwork, and relaxation techniques, you'll cultivate strength, flexibility, and mindfulness to support you through pregnancy and childbirth. Nadya provides a nurturing environment where you can safely explore movement and connect with your growing baby. Join us to embrace the transformative journey of pregnancy with grace and confidence.
