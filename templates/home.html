About Nadya

I discovered the transformative power of yoga during a time
of personal exploration.Drawn to its holistic approach,
I embraced yoga not just as a physical practice but as a journey of self-awareness.
My yoga mat became my sanctuary, a space where I could connect with my breath,
find balance, and nurture both my body and mind.

Classes

My approach to teaching is rooted in creating a supportive and inclusive space.
I believe that yoga is for everyone, regardless of age, flexibility, or experience.
Together, we'll explore the foundations of yoga, celebrate progress over perfection,
and build a community that embraces each individual's unique journey.

Vinyasa Yoga
Experience the fluidity of movement and breath with Vinyasa yoga, a dynamic practice that synchronizes breath with continuous flowing sequences of poses.
Pre-Natal Yoga
Nurture your body and prepare for the miracle of birth with prenatal yoga led by Nadya. Our prenatal yoga classes offer gentle and supportive practices tailored specifically for expectant mothers.
Power Yoga
Ignite your inner fire and unleash your full potential with Power Yoga, a dynamic and vigorous practice that builds strength, stamina, and mental resilience.
