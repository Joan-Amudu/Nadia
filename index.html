About Nadya

Welcome to [Nadya's Yoga], where the journey to wellness begins with a mindful breath and a gentle stretch. I'm Nadya, your guide on this path to self-discovery through the ancient practice of yoga.

I discovered the transformative power of yoga during a time of personal exploration. Drawn to its holistic approach, I embraced yoga not just as a physical practice but as a journey of self-awareness. My yoga mat became my sanctuary, a space where I could connect with my breath, find balance, and nurture both my body and mind.

While I may be a newcomer to teaching, my passion for yoga runs deep. I am eager to share the joy and tranquility that yoga has brought into my life. Every pose, every breath, is an opportunity for self-discovery, and I am excited to embark on this journey with you.

My approach to teaching is rooted in creating a supportive and inclusive space. I believe that yoga is for everyone, regardless of age, flexibility, or experience. Together, we'll explore the foundations of yoga, celebrate progress over perfection, and build a community that embraces each individual's unique journey.
